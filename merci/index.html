Our mission

There is nothing more important than having the word of God planted in your heart, and I mean planting it just the way a farmer intensionally plants his seeds to the ground and patiently monitors their growth. If you plant the word of God consiously and consistently in your heart, it will grow and bear fruits. In Mark 4:14 Jesus said, the sower sows the word. So we have a duty to sow the word of God in our hearts. That is our assignment as believers to diligently sow the word of God into our hearts. We have a guaranty that after we have done this diligently the word of God will bear fruits in our lives, we see that in verse 20. But these are the ones sown on good ground, those who hear the word, accept it, and bear fruit: some thirtyfold, some sixty, and some a hundred. Hallelujah!
Our goal in setting up this website is to help you achieve the task of planting the word of God bit by bit in your heart
